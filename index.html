2026 Projects

Five bets — beyond BAU. The initiatives India Editorial will drive this year.

Now playing — Hot Hits Haryanvi
Project 01 · Q2 2026
Building the Film IP
"The movie never ends on Spotify."
Re-branding catalogue IPs & identifying existing IP for new films. Making Spotify the go-to destination for film music in Hindi and Tamil. New + Catalogue.
Launch: Q2 2026
Project 02 · Q1 2026
Haryanvi Flagship IP
"The genre the mainstream hasn't caught up to yet."
Launch a flagship for Haryanvi music with significant off-platform push. Cash in on the genre's major breakthrough moment. Build association with artists & culture.
Launch: End of Q1 2026
Project 03 · H1 2026
Punjabi Export
"Borders are just a playlist away."
Driving the export of Punjabi music by launching a cross-functional working group. Target diaspora markets: US, CA, UK, AU, MENA. Engage editors globally.
Launch: H1 2026
Project 04 · H2 2026
Project Bhakti
"Devotion has always been India's first music."
Launching a devotional on-platform IP across Hindi, Tamil & Telugu. Fill the spiritual music gap on Spotify India. Build a category that has been underserved.
Launch: H2 2026
Project 05 · Ongoing
Project Echo
"Classical music for the streaming generation."
Continuing work on classical music with a full on-platform ecosystem. Re-position classical for new audiences. Build lasting cultural IPs for India's oldest musical traditions.
Ongoing · 2026
