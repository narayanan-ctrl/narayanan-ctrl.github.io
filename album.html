A · Part One
Conviction
We don't reflect culture. We shape it. India Editorial holds the formative view — the convictions other people eventually catch up to.
A1
Grow the independent. Grow the pie.
In a film-dominated market, the path to growing audience is not louder coverage of the mainstream — it's discovery. Independent artists are how the listening base expands beyond what film soundtracks deliver. Editorial's job is to build the conditions for that discovery, not wait for it to happen.
Proof · RADAR · Rap 91 · IPop Icons
5:12
A2
Indian listening is too top-heavy.
A handful of artists dominate disproportionately. The platform's depth — across language, region, and scene — is not yet reflected in what people actually play. Editorial is the lever that redistributes attention to what deserves it.
[ Proof point — the moment editorial pushed an artist past the top ]
4:48
A3
Depth lives in subcultures.
Sustained listening in India isn't driven by mainstream hits alone. It lives in the regional identities, the communities, the scenes — music tied to memory, season, ceremony. The editor who programmes for community, not just charts, builds something that lasts.
[ Proof point — a community / subculture you've grown ]
